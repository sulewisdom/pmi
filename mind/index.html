About Premium Minds International

Premium Minds International also known as PMI is a leadership training and coaching organisation.  It was founded and born out of the growing need in raising leaders with character globally. In PMI we will be working with both the young and old, young because they are the next generation of leaders and old because we need to explore their wealth of experience, challenges, reasons for some leadership mistakes, successes and support for change.   PMI is a global organisation because it  embraces unity in diversity. A community of people with character and  a sense of hunger for true leadership.  Irrespective of geographical location, we will be connecting with people  through career and skills enhancement,  while talents will be harnessed, enhanced and showcased for true wealth creation. We will be  changing our world by living the change and showing others how to do it.
